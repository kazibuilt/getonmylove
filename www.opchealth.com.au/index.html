    About OPC Health

    For over 40 years OPC Health has provided a dedicated commitment to servicing prosthetic, orthotic, and allied health professionals across Australia & New Zealand.

    As a preferred supplier with major hospitals, allied health services and government bodies, we take great pride in delivering the highest level of service to our customers. Our commitment to excellence continues as we collaborate with health industry innovators, aiming to provide comprehensive solutions for our allied health clients. From physiotherapy to podiatry, hand therapy to osteopathy, you can rely on OPC Health for all our healthcare needs.

    Education & Events

    At OPC Health we endeavour to be not only your supplier, but your clinical partner. With an extensive team of allied health professionals across Australia, we’re always here to help.

    As an organisation, one of our core focuses is advancing knowledge and promoting professional development within our industry. To help fulfil this commitment, we offer diverse educational programs and partner with a range of industry bodies including AOPA, AHTA, APA, ALA and Osteopathy Australia.

    Stay informed about upcoming events including webinars and conferences, educational offerings, and the latest industry news by signing up to our latest updates.
