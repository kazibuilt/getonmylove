PRODUCTS

ORTHOTICS

Our specialized production centers allow us to offer you a complete range of light orthopedic products. Thanks to our experience as technicians in different fields of orthopedic manufacturing, we can guarantee you a product of the highest quality.

FOOTWEAR

EMO footwear is made with one objective: to strike a balance between technique and aesthetics. Of course, the technical component is essential to solve the pathologies of your patients. But without the aesthetic component, it is very difficult for the patient to have continuity in the treatment.

MASTECTOMY

Our mastectomy team draws on its technical knowledge to improve the quality of life for post-mastectomized women through two product lines. Cisne's experience leads us to innovation at Alina, both concerned with reinforcing such important aspects as femininity and comfort of use.

TECHNICAL AIDS

EMO's technical quality is a constant in all product lines. The knowledge and experience acquired over the years make it possible to offer products in continuous evolution. Driven by the vocation of the EMO team, always focused on finding innovative solutions that improve the quality of life of patients.

PROSTHETICS

Having a wide range of prosthetic solutions at your disposal is not enough for EMO. The important thing is to give you technical support whenever you need it. Guarantee you a comprehensive service thanks to our geographical proximity and above all, our experience in the technical workshop.

SUPPLIES

Working in our orthopedic workshop for so many years gives us a deep understanding of the machinery and raw materials needed. The next step: share that experience with you. We are not satisfied with supplying you with supplies, we also give you technical support so that you can develop your own workshop.

CENTRAL FAB

Tu negocio necesita de ti y de tu tiempo. Conoce los detalles.

EMO, after its trajectory and experience in the prosthetics and orthotics sector, gathers all its resources to offer a service of products 100% personalized to the needs of all clients. A service that ranges from manufacturing with traditional systems to CAD-CAM systems. The technical knowledge and experience, the possibilities of our organization and the availability of a wide range of materials and finishes, allow us to offer you quality support and, in turn, have more time to deal with what is truly important: dedicated and meticulous care of your patients, which also adds value to your business.
